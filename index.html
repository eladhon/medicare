About Medicare

Medicare is a fast, clinically accurate drug information platform designed to provide clean, accessible medical knowledge.

This application integrates real-time data from the U.S. Food & Drug Administration (FDA) and the National Library of Medicine (NLM) RxNorm API to deliver smart predictions and comprehensive drug labeling.
